Biography

I am an assistant professor at the School of Computing, KyungHee University, Republic of Korea. Prior to joining KyungHee University, I served as a research intern at Amazon Science in New York. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Data Mining and Information Systems Lab in the Computer Science department at Korea University, Korea. My research interests include Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. I am particularly passionate about applying AI technologies to solve real-world challenges, especially in biomedicine and science. Lately, my research has been on knowledge extraction, information retrieval, and question answering.
